Rules & Fair Play

Team & Player Requirements

  • Team Captains: Primary contact for communication & prize distribution; responsible for their team’s conduct.
  • Player Registration: All 5 members must be officially registered.

Prohibited Actions

  • Event troops
  • Multiple accounts

Results & Disputes

  • Result Submission: Winning team must submit a screenshot of final result; false submissions result in immediate disqualification.
  • Disputes: Organizers may check clan war logs directly.

Match Scheduling & Gameplay

  • Match Structure: 10 minutes preparation, 30 minutes battle.
  • Scheduling: Captains must contact opponents to arrange match time; if no response in 24 hours → automatic advance.
Penalties
  • Attack after time = 1‑star penalty
  • 15 minutes late = 1‑star penalty
  • 30 minutes late = forfeit (opponent advances)
